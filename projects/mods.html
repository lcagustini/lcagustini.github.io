Crystal Gun

Personal work

A Minecraft 1.5 mod introducing a variety of new features:

  • Magical guns that inflict various status effects.
  • An enhanced potion system utilizing cauldrons.
  • Defense turrets for improved base security.
  • Simple automation blocks to streamline tasks.

This mod enriches the gameplay experience with new mechanics and tools.

Counter Strike Maps

Personal work

A collection of basic CS:GO maps created primarily as a learning exercise using the Hammer Editor and Source Engine.

These maps were designed to explore level design principles and the technical aspects of map creation within the CS:GO environment.
